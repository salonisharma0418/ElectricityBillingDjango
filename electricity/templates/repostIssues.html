Report an Issue/Bug

At Photospot Billing, we strive to provide a seamless experience for all our users. However, we understand that sometimes issues or bugs may arise. Your feedback is crucial in helping us improve our services. If you encounter any issues or bugs while using our website or services, please report them using the form below.

How to Report an Issue/Bug

To help us address your issue as quickly as possible, please provide the following information:

  1. Your Contact Information:
    • Name
    • Email Address
    • Phone Number (optional)
  2. Issue Details:
    • Describe the issue or bug in detail.
    • Provide steps to reproduce the issue, if possible.
    • Include any error messages you received.
    • Mention the date and time when the issue occurred.
  3. Technical Information:
    • Browser type and version
    • Operating system
    • Device type (e.g., desktop, mobile, tablet)
  4. Screenshots/Attachments:
    • If applicable, please attach screenshots or any other files that can help us understand the issue better.

Steps to Reproduce:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Go to the "Make a Payment" page.
  3. Enter payment details.
  4. Click on "Proceed to Confirmation."
